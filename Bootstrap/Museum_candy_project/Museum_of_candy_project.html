MUSEUM OF CANDY

The notion of sweet is used to describe what causes a pleasant and subtle effect on the palate. It is about the taste that, in comparison, is not salty, bitter or sour. It is also often known as sweets to certain foods that are characterized by this type of flavor, such as jams and candies or sweets.
